Bio

Adrian Morawdi is a young independent scholar and literature instructor whose Research focuses on interdisciplinary literary theory and quantum approaches to Literature. His work explores the integration of quantum physics with innovative, Non-traditional and disruptive perspectives in literary theory, and he is particularly Interested in teaching literature through new, experimental and forward-looking Pedagogical methods.
