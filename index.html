Adrian Morawdi is the pen name of Alireza Moradi, an emerging interdisciplinary literary Researcher and literature instructor based in Iran. His academic work centers on innovative Approaches to literary interpretation, particularly where literary theory intersects with Broader epistemological and interdisciplinary questions. His intellectual interests include Comparative literary studies, postmodernism, poetics, aesthetics, and original theoretical Inquiry into the relationship between literature and nontraditional conceptual frameworks.

Alongside research and teaching, he actively contributes to literary culture through Educational content creation, poetry performance, reading circles, and academic discussion sessions.
